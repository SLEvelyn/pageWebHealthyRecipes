Protein Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Protein Scoop
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • 2 Tbsp Coconut Oil
  • 1 Banana
  • Chocolate chips to taste

Preparation

Place a mashed banana in a bowl, add the coconut oil, cinnamon and mix very well. Then we add the oats, the protein, the chocolate pieces and mix. We form the cookies and put them in a preheated oven at 180 C Â° for 20 minutes and that's it.
