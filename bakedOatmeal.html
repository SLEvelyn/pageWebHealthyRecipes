Baked Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 40 g Rolled oats
  • 1/2 Banana
  • 120 ml Almond milk
  • 1 Tsp cinnamon
  • 1 Tsp Baking powder
  • Blackberries
  • Strawberries (Toppings)
  • Papaya (Toppings)
>

Preparation

We preheat the oven to high and low heat at 175ºC. - In two small oven-safe containers, mix all the necessary ingredients: the mashed banana, the rolled oats, the almond milk, the baking powder, the cinnamon and the blackberries.
Bake on a wire rack for 15 minutes at 175ºC with heat only on the top until golden brown. Remove from oven and let cool. Decorate with strawberries and papaya on top or whatever you like the most and enjoy it.
