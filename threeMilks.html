Three Milks

Ingredients

    Cake
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Almond Flour (100 gr)
  • 2 Tbsp Coconut Oil (30 ml)
  • Sweetener to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder (5 gr)
    • Milk mix
  • 60 ml Coconut milk
  • 60 ml Almond milk
  • Sweetener to taste
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
    • Coverage
  • 45 gr Greek yogurt
  • Sweetener to taste
  • 15 ml Vanilla Extract

Preparation

Cake: In a bowl we put the eggs and beat very well, we add the oil, the vanilla extract, the sweetener and the yeast, mix very well, to gradually add the almond flour; It is normal for the mixture to be a little dense, since it is ready, we place the mixture in a small mold and in the oven preheated to 180 C Â° for 15 minutes, the time will depend on your oven, to check if the cake is ready, insert a toothpick and if it comes out clean, that's it.


Milk mix:To bathe the cake, we place in a bowl all the ingredients already mentioned for the mixture of the milk, mix very well and proceed to pierce the cake with a toothpick or with a fork, pour the mixture on the cake and let it rest for 2 hours or all night.


Coverage:In a bowl we add Greek yogurt, sweetener to taste and vanilla and mix gently. We take the cake out of the fridge and place the delicious prepared coverage, let it rest a bit, put some blueberries and a little granola and that's it. Enjoy it.
