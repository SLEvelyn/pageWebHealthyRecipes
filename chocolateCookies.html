Chocolate Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Cup Almond fluor
  • 1/3 Cup Melted chocolate or powder cacao
  • 1 Egg
  • 4 Tbsp Coconut Oil
  • Sweetener to taste
  • 1 Tsp Baking powder
  • A little milk in case the mixture does not compact.
  • Chocolate chips to taste

Preparation

Put all the ingredients in a bowl, mix until it forms a homogeneous dough that is not sticky or lumpy to be able to form the cookies.
Make small balls and shape it into a cookie, place chocolate chips on top, bake for 25 minutes at 200 Â° C and enjoy.
