Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Banana
  • 2 Egg Whites
  • 25g Oatmeal
  • 2 Chocolate Chips
  • Blackberries to taste
  • Strawberries to taste
  • Peanut butter to garnish

Preparation

Put 1/2 banana, 2 egg whites, 25 g of oats in a food processor and blend very well. Then in a nonstick skillet over low heat, add a little of the mixture to the pan, add a blackberry and place a little more of the mixture to cover the blackberry.
Do the same process but in two place a piece of chocolate and so you will have two pancakes filled with chocolate and two with blackberry. Toppings to taste like strawberries, more blackberries and peanut butter and enjoy.
